Daily Purchasing

Raw Material Purchase Cart

Add raw materials to a purchase cart first. After all items are added, tap Complete Purchase, enter one delivery fee for all items, then save. This updates inventory and records the raw material expense.

Example: Buns → Pack → No. of packs: 1 → Pieces per pack: 6 → Price: ₱35. Inventory adds 6 pieces at ₱5.83 each.
